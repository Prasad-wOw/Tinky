Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Tinky is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency about how information is processed within the application.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Tinky processes, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

1. Privacy-First Design

Tinky is designed with a privacy-first approach.

Tinky does not continuously analyze, store, or transmit everything you type.

Text is processed only when you intentionally activate a Tinky feature, including:

  • Trigger commands (e.g. .g, .tr, .polite)
  • AI popup shortcuts
  • Grammar correction
  • Translation
  • Summarization
  • Explanation requests
  • Custom AI prompts

2. Information We Process

Tinky may process:

  • Selected text submitted for AI processing
  • User prompts and custom instructions
  • Application settings and preferences
  • Configured AI provider information
  • License and activation information (if applicable)
  • Diagnostic information required for troubleshooting

3. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

  • Passwords
  • Banking credentials
  • Credit card information
  • Government-issued identification numbers
  • Keystrokes unrelated to activated Tinky actions

Tinky is designed to avoid processing secure input fields whenever technically possible.

4. Clipboard Access

Certain features require temporary clipboard access.

When clipboard access is required:

  • Clipboard contents may be temporarily read.
  • The requested operation is performed.
  • The original clipboard contents are restored whenever possible.

Clipboard contents are not permanently stored.

5. Temporary History Storage

Tinky may temporarily store transformation history locally to support:

  • Undo functionality
  • History recovery
  • Recent activity viewing

History data may include:

  • Original text
  • Generated text
  • Timestamp
  • Action type

History is stored locally on the user's device and automatically removed after the configured retention period.

6. AI Provider Processing

When AI-powered features are used, selected text may be transmitted to the AI provider chosen by the user.

Supported providers may include:

  • Google Gemini
  • OpenAI
  • OpenRouter
  • Cloudflare Workers AI
  • Ollama (local processing)

Processing by third-party AI providers is governed by their respective privacy policies and terms of service.

7. Local AI Processing

When local AI providers such as Ollama are used, text processing occurs on the user's device and is not transmitted to external AI services unless explicitly configured by the user.

8. API Keys

API keys may be stored locally to enable AI provider integration.

  • Stored locally on the user's device
  • Used only for provider access
  • Not sold or shared by Tinky
  • Encrypted where supported

9. Licensing and Activation

If licensing features are enabled, Tinky may process:

  • License identifiers
  • Activation status
  • Device information necessary for validation

This information is used solely for activation, fraud prevention, and license verification.

10. Security

Tinky uses reasonable security measures including:

  • Encrypted communications where applicable
  • Local storage protections
  • API key protection mechanisms
  • Access controls

No system can guarantee absolute security.

11. User Controls

Users may:

  • Delete local history
  • Remove API keys
  • Disable AI providers
  • Adjust privacy settings
  • Uninstall the application

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date shown at the top of this page.

13. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Tinky Support
Email: support@tinkyapp.com

Privacy Summary
  • Tinky only processes text when you explicitly invoke a feature.
  • Tinky does not permanently store your text by default.
  • Temporary history is stored locally to support undo and recovery.
  • Selected text may be sent to your chosen AI provider.
  • You remain in control of your settings, data, and AI providers.
