Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tinky. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. Tinky is designed with a privacy-first architecture, ensuring that your data remains securely on your device.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Tinky does NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. The application operates completely locally on your machine, with the following operational behaviors:

  • Text Processing: When you invoke a trigger, Tinky temporarily reads the selected text to send it to the AI provider you have explicitly configured (e.g., OpenRouter, OpenAI, Gemini).
  • Local Storage: All settings, API keys, custom triggers, and conversation history are encrypted and stored locally on your device in your AppData folder.
  • No Telemetry: We do not track your usage, keystrokes, or application behavior.

3. Third-Party AI Providers

To provide AI generation capabilities, Tinky communicates directly with the Third-Party AI APIs you configure (such as OpenAI, Google Gemini, or OpenRouter). By using Tinky, you agree that your selected text and prompts will be transmitted directly to these third-party services.

We strongly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of the AI providers you choose to connect to Tinky.

4. Security

Your API keys are encrypted at rest using machine-specific encryption keys. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

5. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Information

To ask questions or comment about this privacy policy and our privacy practices, please contact the developer via the official repository or website.
