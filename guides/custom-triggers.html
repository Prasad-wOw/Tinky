What is a Trigger?

A trigger is a short text snippet (usually ending with a dot, like .pirate) that tells Tinky to perform a specific AI instruction on the text you just typed.

Instead of copying text, opening a browser, prompting ChatGPT, and pasting the result back... you just type the trigger right in your app!

2

🛠️
Create Your Trigger

Let's make a custom trigger that turns any boring message into pirate speak.

  1. Open the Tinky Dashboard.
  2. Click on the Settings gear icon.
  3. Navigate to the Triggers tab.
  4. Click Add New Trigger.
  5. Fill in the details as shown below, then hit Save.

Trigger String

.pirate

AI Instruction

Rewrite this text in the style of a pirate. Arrr!

See the Magic Happen

Your custom trigger is ready to be used in Discord, Word, VS Code, or any other app!

  1. Open any text editor or chat app.
  2. Type your message: Hello team, let's start the meeting.
  3. Type your trigger right after: .pirate
  4. Watch as Tinky automatically replaces it with: Ahoy mateys! Let's hoist the sails and begin this parley.
Pro Tip: You can create triggers for anything! E.g. .code (Refactor this code), .polite (Make this email sound professional), or .translate (Translate to Spanish).
